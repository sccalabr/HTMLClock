Established in 1978, the Cal Poly American Marketing Association chapter has been connecting students to their peers, professors, community, and potential industry employers for over 25 years. As a nationally recognized organization the American Marketing Association aims to provide those interested in the field of marketing as well as business in general with the tools and tips needed to create a successful career and future. As one of Cal Poly's largest and oldest professional club, AMA prides itself on our ability to bring top level companies and businesses to our campus as well as giving insight into the vast depth of career opportunities related to marketing. Not only do we work hard to ensure our members success, but we work hard to build last relationships and have fun because that is marketing is all about. We love what we do and will continue to fuel the future of marketing with our innovative and driven members entering the business world everyday.

What is AMA?

"The American Marketing Association (AMA) is the world's largest and most comprehensive professional society of marketers, consisting of more than 45,000 worldwide members in 92 countries and 500 chapters throughout North America. The AMA is the only organization that provides direct benefits to marketing professionals in both business and education and assist in personal and professional career development among marketing professionals, and to advance the science and ethical practice of marketing disciplines."

Mission Statement

The American Marketing Association is a professional association for individuals and organizations involved in the practice, teaching and study of marketing worldwide.

Our principal roles include:

Improving - Advancing marketing competencies, practice and thought
leadership.

Promoting - Being an advocate for marketing and promoting its importance,
efficacy and ethics.

Supporting - Being an essential resource for marketing information,
education/training and relationships.
